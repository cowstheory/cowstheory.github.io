Cows Theory

The year is 1958 and the space race between the Soviet Union and the USA is in full swing. In 1957, the Soviet Union sent the dog Laika to space. Now, both nations are moving on to the next step in the space race: sending cows to space! The steaks have never been higher as the whole world has set their eyes on the creation of the SPACE Program for Astronomic Cattle Establishment, 'SPACE'. Everybody wants to be a part of the new intergalactic revolution, and soon enough both Japan and Europe are sure to join the battle. The war of a lifetime is taking place in space. Whoever conquers the war may very well conquer the universe...

About

Cows theory is a physics based 2.5D shooter created in Unity. The goal is to push the opponent cow(s) into the black hole in the middle. The black hole also affects the cows by gravitational pull. As the cows take more damage, the effect from the black hole increases - making it harder to not get spagetthified.
